Mood Tracking Tips

Benefits of Mood Tracking
  • Identify mood patterns and triggers
  • Recognize early warning signs of mental health changes
  • Measure progress in emotional well-being
  • Provides data to share with mental health professionals
How to Use the Tracker
  • Track once daily for best results
  • Be honest about how you're feeling
  • Include notes about what influenced your mood
  • Review your trends weekly and monthly
When to Seek Help

If you notice consistent low moods for more than two weeks, or sudden extreme changes in mood, consider reaching out to a mental health professional.
